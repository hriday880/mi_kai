# Compliance and SBOM
## Overview
Inventory of open-source licenses and open dependencies.

## Findings
- The project uses standard MIT/Apache-2.0 licensed libraries typical in the React/Next.js ecosystem.
- No copyleft licenses (e.g., GPL) were immediately identified that would enforce open-sourcing of proprietary code.

## Recommendations
- Generate a formal Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) using tools like Syft during the CI pipeline.
- Regularly review licensing of newly added dependencies to ensure compliance with company policies.
